## 3 · Minute-by-minute facilitator script

### 🔥 10:55 - 11:00 · Pattern Interrupt (Eker)

**You DO:** be on camera · standing · NOT seated. Music low volume · upbeat instrumental.

**You SAY** (verbatim, slow, eyes locked):

> *"Stop. Before we touch a single keyboard. I want you to know something."*
>
> *"Everyone in this Zoom right now believes one of two stories about themselves. Story A · 'I'm a founder building something real.' Story B · 'I'm faking it · the real ones are somewhere else.'"*
>
> *"Most of you are running Story B. That's why you registered for a $49 workshop. You wanted to know if it's real. It's real."*

**Beat. 3-second silence.**

> *"For the next two hours · we delete Story B. We don't delete it by talking · we delete it by doing. By the end of today · you will have published a LinkedIn post in your voice · and 3 cold outreach messages will be queued in your Gmail. Both written by AI · both sounding exactly like you · both done before lunch."*

**Drop in chat:**
```
🔥 Day 1 starts NOW. Workbook: https://studio-style-design-workshop.vercel.app/workbook/day-1/
```

---

### ⚡ 11:00 - 11:03 · State-shift opener (Singer)

**You SAY:**

> *"Cameras on. Everyone stand up. RIGHT NOW. I'm not joking · stand. If you're in a coffee shop · stand. Yes, people will look. Let them."*

Wait until 80% of cameras show standing.

> *"Hands above your head. Higher. Take the deepest breath you've taken today. Hold for 3. Exhale loud · I want to hear it."*

3-second hold · everyone exhales together.

> *"Hands down. Look at your camera. Now say this OUT LOUD with me · three times · loud enough that you'd be embarrassed if anyone walked in. Ready?"*

> ***"By 13:00 today · my business has shipped its first AI post."***
> ***"By 13:00 today · my business has shipped its first AI post."***
> ***"BY 13:00 TODAY · MY BUSINESS HAS SHIPPED ITS FIRST AI POST."***

**Sit.** *"Good. State managed. Now we work."*

---

### 📜 11:03 - 11:05 · The Code of Honor (Singer)

**Drop in chat:**
```
THE CODE OF HONOR · 5 AGREEMENTS for the next 2 days · type "I AGREE" to all 5

1. Camera on · all 2 hours · except for the bathroom
2. If I'm stuck · I post a screenshot in chat in 60 seconds
3. If someone else is stuck · I help them before I move on
4. I do not type "this won't work for me" · I type "what's the fix"
5. By Sunday 13:00 · I have a yes / not-yet / no on AI CEO Platform

Type "I AGREE" if you're in.
```

**Wait** until 80% of attendees have typed "I AGREE" before continuing.

**You SAY:** *"This is the Code. Anyone who doesn't agree · please drop off and email me for a refund. No hard feelings. We need everyone in or no one in. Code of Honor is what makes the next 2 hours work."*

### 11:05 - 11:08 · Money Blueprint (Eker)

**You SAY:**

> *"Before we open Claude Code · one question. Type your answer in chat · don't think about it · 10 seconds."*
>
> *"What's the LIE you've been telling yourself about your business? The one you say to yourself at 2am when you can't sleep. Six words or less."*

Watch chat. Read 3-4 aloud · NO commentary · just read.

Examples to expect:
- *"I'm not technical enough"*
- *"It's too late for me"*
- *"My voice doesn't matter"*
- *"I'll never make 50K MRR"*

> *"That lie is your Money Blueprint. The story you inherited · from a parent · from a teacher · from a failed launch. It runs your business. Today · we don't fight it. We just give your business its OWN voice · so the lie has nowhere to land."*
>
> *"That's what build-my-brain is. It's not a setup script. It's the moment your business stops running on YOUR blueprint and starts running on its own."*

**Now → Foundation activity (`/studio-style-design:build-my-brain`).** Frame shift: from *"I'm running an install"* to *"I'm naming my voice."*

---

### 11:45 - 11:50 · Declaration Anchor (Eker · between Foundation and Activity 1)

After build-my-brain finishes · BEFORE marketing-engine runs:

**You SAY:**

> *"Stop. Hand on your heart. Right hand. ON YOUR HEART. I'm watching."*

3-second pause. Wait for compliance.

> *"Repeat after me · with feeling · three times:"*
>
> ***"My voice is mine. My business has my voice. My business writes for me."***
> ***"My voice is mine. My business has my voice. My business writes for me."***
> ***"MY VOICE IS MINE. MY BUSINESS HAS MY VOICE. MY BUSINESS WRITES FOR ME."***

> *"Hand down. Now we run marketing-engine · and you watch your business write a post in YOUR voice. That's the moment Story B dies."*

**Why this works:** declaration BEFORE evidence. The post Claude writes lands as **confirmation of the declaration**, not as a surprise. That's Eker's fundamental trick.

### 12:05 - 12:10 · Little Voice exercise (Singer · between Activity 1 and 2)

After they have their post · BEFORE sales-engine:

**You SAY:**

> *"Look at the post your business just wrote. Read it once. Now I want you to listen for the Little Voice."*
>
> *"The Little Voice is the saboteur. Right now it's saying one of three things:"*
>
> *"1. 'It's not as good as the influencers.'"*
> *"2. 'No one will care.'"*
> *"3. 'What will [parent / spouse / ex-boss] think?'"*
>
> *"Type in chat which one it is. Just the number."*

Watch chat. Most type 1 or 3.

> *"That voice doesn't go away. We don't kill it. We just outproduce it. The deal we make today is: when the Little Voice shows up tomorrow · you publish ANYWAY. The system is the rebellion."*

> *"Now sales-engine. Your business is about to find 10 people who actually need what you do."*

### 12:50 - 12:55 · Public Commitment (Singer · before Day 1 close)

After Activity 4 wraps:

**You SAY:**

> *"Stand up. Yes again. We're closing strong."*

Wait for cameras to show standing.

> *"I'm going to call on 5 of you · randomly · BY NAME. When I call your name · you say one sentence · cameras off if you're shy is fine but voice ON. The sentence is:"*
>
> ***"By Sunday at 13:00, my business will have ____."***
>
> *"Fill the blank. One thing. Specific. The number, the deliverable, the date. Ready?"*

Pick 5 names from your attendee list at random. Wait for each declaration. Don't comment · just say *"Heard. Next: [name]."*

After 5:

> *"Everyone else · type yours in chat. ONE sentence. Right now."*

Watch the chat fill.

> *"This is the Code activated. By Sunday 13:00, the people who said it out loud and the people who typed it · we hold each other to it."*

### 12:55 - 13:00 · Anchor Close (Eker)

**You SAY:**

> *"Last 4 minutes. Right hand on your heart. Left hand pointing at your camera."*

Wait.

> *"Everyone breathe in. 3 seconds. Hold. Exhale."*
>
> *"Look at the post in your LinkedIn drafts. Look at the 3 outreach messages in your Gmail drafts. Look at your calendar with 6 routines on it."*
>
> *"That wasn't there at 11:00. It is now. Two hours."*
>
> *"Tomorrow at 11:00 we close the loop · operations, cash, leadership, the AI CEO platform decision. Until then · ONE thing · publish that LinkedIn post. Schedule it · or send it · before you sleep tonight. The post · in your voice · public."*
>
> *"Hand down. Day 1 wraps. See you at 11:00 tomorrow. Bring receipts."*

**Hard close.** Don't say "any questions". Don't say "feel free to email me". Just close. Let them sit with it.

## 4 · Why this works (the mechanism)

Singer + Eker both teach the same core sequence:

```
STATE → IDENTITY → DECLARATION → PROOF → ANCHOR
```

The existing Day 1 had **Proof** (the activities) but missed the other 4. Adding them takes Day 1 from *"a workshop where they ran some commands"* to *"the day my business changed."*

The declarations matter most. The brain remembers what the body did · NOT what the slides said. Hand-on-heart + spoken phrase + 3 repetitions creates a state anchor that fires every Monday at 7am when their alarm goes off and they paste `/studio-style-design:marketing-engine`.

That's how you turn one workshop into 6 months of compound LinkedIn posts.

## 7 · Recovery scripts · if a beat doesn't land

| Failure mode | Symptom | Recovery |
|---|---|---|
| Cameras stay off when you say "stand" | <50% standing after 10s | "I see 8 cameras off. I'm not moving until those cameras come on. The investment you made for this $49 spot only works if you're IN. Cameras." |
| Chat is empty after you ask for the lie | <30% engagement | "Type ANYTHING. Even 'idk' counts. Movement is the goal. 5-4-3-2-1." |
| Someone resists the declaration | Visible eye-roll on cam | "I see you. The eye-roll IS the Little Voice. Say it anyway. The body remembers what the eye-roll resists." |
| The 5 named people freeze | Long silence after name called | Wait 10s. Then: "[name], take a breath. Just say what you want by Sunday. One sentence. We've got you." |
| Tech fails mid-workshop (Zoom drops) | You disconnect | DM Karthik immediately to keep room calm. Reconnect within 2 min. Open with: "Tech happens. The Code of Honor still holds. Where were we?" |

## 8 · After the workshop · between Day 1 and Day 2

Saturday 13:00 · within 30 minutes of close · do this:

1. **Personal voice note** to each person who DIDN'T type in chat at 12:55. *"Hey [name] · I noticed you didn't share your Sunday goal in chat. No judgment. Just want to know if you're in for tomorrow. Voice note me yes or no?"* This is Singer's "no one falls through" rule.

2. **Saturday evening 18:00 Finland** · post in workshop Slack channel: *"Daily check · who has scheduled their LinkedIn post for tomorrow 7am? Drop the screenshot."* This is Eker's "60-second tracking" principle. Public accountability.

3. **Saturday 22:00** · personal DM to top 5 highest-engagement attendees. Ask one question: *"On a scale of 1-10 · how likely are you to consider AI CEO Platform tomorrow?"* This pre-qualifies the 50-min pitch on Day 2 · you go in knowing who's already an 8+ vs who needs the full close.
